kim jonghyun

the poet, the artist

timeline:

  • 1990 - born in Seoul, South Korea
  • 2005 - scouted by SM entertainment when he was 15 years old thanks to his angelic voice and striking visuals. prepares to train with labelmates for debut.
  • 2008 - debuted as part of the five member boy group Shinee, along with Onew, Key, Minho and Taemin.
  • 2011 - competed on KBS' Immortal Songs: Singing The Legend. won first round against labelmate Super Junior's Yesung.
  • 2013 - debuted as a composer with the track 'A Gloomy Clock', featured on IU's third korean album 'Modern Times'
  • 2015 - made his solo debut with his first EP, 'Base'which received critical acclaim, topping Billboard World Album Charts
  • 2016 - released his first solo album, 'She Is', most tracks of which were written and composed by Jonghyun.
  • 2017 - held his last concert titled 'Inspired' in South Korea
  • 2017 - died of suicide by carbon monoxide.
  • 2018 - final album 'Poet | Artist' released posthumously along with the 'Shinin' music video.
